FROM PRINCIPAL'S DESK
Greetings from the Principal of Hansraj College! As a premier instituion of higher education, Hansraj College offers its students a unique blend of modern education anchored in hallowed Vedic values. The college is quided in its mission of combining the best of tradition and modernity by the vision of Mahatma Hans Raj. It is in the memory of Mahatma ji, a pioneer in the field of education, that the D.A.V. College Management Committee had founded the college on 26th July, 1948. The college has been contributing to the solemn task of nation-building since its very inception.
